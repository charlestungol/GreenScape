Irrigation System Frequently Asked Questions 
What is your contact number? 
You may contact us via phone at 403-255-6396 
How much does a system cost? 
There are a number of factors that determine the cost of an irrigation system, including 
number of zones determined by landscape layout, water pressure and flow. While not all 
cases are the same, the typical cost ends up to around $800 per zone on systems with a 
minimum of three zones. 
Do you do landscape construction? 
No, irrigation and landscape lighting is all we do. We do however work alongside some of 
Calgary’s best landscapers, and would be happy to refer you to them. 
How long does it take to install a residential irrigation system? 
Usually the installation takes only one day, but in some cases additional time may be 
necessary. 
Will an automatic sprinkler system use more water than I am currently using? 
No — in fact an automatic sprinkler system may actually save you water. A well designed, 
efficient irrigation system applies exactly the amount of water needed by your landscape. 
My property is already sodded and landscaped. If I get an irrigation system 
installed, won’t it ruin all my grass and plants? 
We install our pipe with a vibrating plough that’s minimally invasive to sod. Sod at sprinkler 
heads and pipe connections is carefully replaced afterwards. We take pride in our ability to 
install a system and make it seem like no one was ever there. 
Will an automatic sprinkler system water as well as I do when I water by hand? 
An automatic sprinkler system will do a more efficient job because it will be designed based 
on your landscape’s diverse watering needs, using specific products designed to water grass, 
trees, shrubs or garden plants. 
I have a well. Can I still have an irrigation system? 
Certainly. We specialize in pump drawn systems for well-derived water supplies. With 
today’s advanced technology, there are numerous options available. 
Is there any upkeep involved after installation? 
Our service schedule includes a spring turn-on and a winter shut-down. We will contact you 
to schedule both of these appointments. 
Will the irrigation system still run when it’s raining? 
The installation of an automatic rain sensor is standard on systems we install and will 
ensure that after rainfall has accumulated, the system will temporarily stop the automatic 
watering cycle. 
Do I need to obtain a permit to install a lawn sprinkler system? 
No permits are required for the installation of a lawn sprinkler system in Calgary. 
When is it best to install the lawn sprinkler system? 
Installation can take place whenever frost is out of the ground – typically from April to 
October. However installations can take place at any time. 
Will I save money if I install my own system? 
Possibly. In fact, it could cost you more money in the long run. A professionally installed 
irrigation system will be designed and installed using the most cost-effective and 
energy-efficient methods possible. 
Where do I shut off my system? 
Turn controller to off. Then locate water shut off in the basement. Turn handle from parallel 
to water line to 90 degrees perpendicular to water line.

Company Information & Services 
Installation & Service – Residential Installations 
From a simple front yard to sprawling acreages no job is too big or too small. Typically an 
underground sprinkler system can be installed in one to two days. After cleaning up your 
yard we will finish the job with a quick inspection and final adjustment of the sprinkler 
system. Upon completion a representative from the company will take you through the 
operation, watering schedule and maintenance requirements of your new system. We will 
then train you on the controller so you are set to operate and enjoy your new sprinkler 
system. 
Installation & Service – Commercial Installations 
Commercial installations can cover a large array of projects including plazas, apartment 
buildings, strip malls, office buildings, industrial properties, parks and golf courses. Each 
project is approached independently to analyze unique requirements such as property size, 
landscaped areas, conduits under roadways, available water (GPM) and supply. Our certified 
designers and technicians have worked on major systems incorporating rainfall harvesting 
and storm water management.

EZ-FLO Mainline Fertilization 
Fertigation is the technique of applying fertilizer through irrigation water. It is sustainable 
and environmentally beneficial, reducing pollution and saving water. EZ-FLO equipment 
delivers liquid or water-soluble products including mosquito repellant. Systems can connect 
to hose bibs, drip systems, or irrigation systems. Greenscape is a certified provider and 
installer of EZ-FLO mainline fertigation. 
Landscape Lighting 
Landscape lighting serves security, visual appeal, and beauty. Low voltage (12V) lighting is 
safer and more economical than 120V lighting. Systems can include timers, photocells, and 
motion sensors. Enhance your home's curb appeal while adding security. 
Rainwater Harvesting / Stormwater Management / Evaporation 
Rainwater harvesting captures and utilizes rainfall instead of funneling it into storm drains. 
Captured rainwater can be reused for irrigation with minimal pre-treatment. Stormwater 
systems can incorporate WiFi integration, flow sensors, soil sensors, and real-time 
monitoring. Greenscape designs and installs rainwater reuse systems for residential and 
commercial projects. 
Certified Stormwater Management Designs 
Our accredited designers create custom stormwater management systems for residential 
and commercial installations including parks, golf courses, and large-scale developments. 
Our systems focus on efficiency, sustainability, and cost reduction through advanced 
technologies.
